The Solution

How It Works

Grub.io is a full-stack mobile application built with React Native, Firebase, and Expo that tackles food waste at its source — events. When an event ends and food remains, attendees can open the app and list surplus items with descriptions, making them instantly available to the local community.

The app leverages Firebase for real-time database synchronization, ensuring that food listings appear immediately for nearby users. Each listing generates a unique QR code that facilitates secure, verified pickup. In-app notifications keep users informed when new food becomes available, creating a responsive ecosystem where surplus food finds a home quickly. The entire experience is designed to be frictionless, encouraging participation and maximizing the amount of food rescued from waste.
