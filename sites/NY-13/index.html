The Tool

How It Works

Trash Bin Optimizer ingests NYC public datasets including pedestrian traffic counts and litter basket placement data. Using pandas for data wrangling and geopandas for geographic operations, the tool performs spatial joins and coverage analysis to identify areas where foot traffic is high but bin density is low.

The analysis pipeline uses scipy for spatial computations such as nearest-neighbor searches and coverage radius calculations. Results are presented through an interactive Streamlit dashboard where users can explore Folium-generated maps, filter by neighborhood or traffic threshold, and view ranked recommendations for new bin placements. The tool is designed for city planners, community boards, and civic organizations looking to make data-informed decisions about urban waste infrastructure.
