How It Works

Connecting Engineers and Citizens

CivicLink is a web platform built to solve a real problem in local government: the disconnect between civic engineers planning infrastructure changes and the citizens affected by those changes. Too often, road closures, utility upgrades, and construction projects happen with little advance notice or clear communication to residents.

The platform provides civic engineers with a straightforward interface to post updates about local infrastructure projects, including timelines, affected areas, and project details. Citizens can then access these updates through a clean, accessible website, staying informed about what is happening in their city and how it may impact their daily routines. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, CivicLink prioritizes simplicity and usability for both engineers and the public.
